How to Embed a YouTube Video

This tutorial will guide you through embedding a video into your website.

Step 1: Find Your Video

Go to YouTube and choose a video that allows embedding. In this example, we used a Beethoven classical music video.

Step 2: Click the Share Button

Click the 'Share' button below the video player to access the embed options.

Step 3: Select Embed and Copy the Code

Click the 'Embed' button and copy the iframe code provided in the pop-up window.

Step 4: Paste the Embed Code in Your HTML

Open your HTML file and paste the iframe code inside the <body> tag where you want the video to appear.
